Chester Bennington Tribute

Breif Bio

Chester Bennington was the lead vocalist in an alterative rock band called Linkin Park and later started his own band called Dead by Sunlight. Throughout his career in Linkin Park they release several albums, since 2004. The primary releases include: Hybrid Theory, Meteora, and Minutes to Midnight.

Chester was a singer, song writer and a tatoo enthusiast. He was not very politically outspoken but following a tragic event, Chester responded publicly saying "There's a non-violent way to express yourself and get your point across â€“ regardless of what you're saying or what your point is. In a free society, people have a right to believe whatever they want to believe. That's their business and they can speak their mind. But nobody, even in a free society, has the right to take another person's life. Ever. That's something that we really need to move beyond." Chester suffered from intense depression and masked this with substance abuse. Tragically, he took his life in July of 2017.
